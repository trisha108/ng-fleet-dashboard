How EV swap priority is calculated
Each ICE vehicle that is EV-capable (light-duty vans, pickups, SUVs) receives a priority score based on: percentage of useful life consumed (60% weight) + estimated annual fuel cost saved by switching (40% weight). High-priority vehicles should be targeted first in the next procurement cycle.
